Right Beside You

In Hush Hollow the garden went slow and gold, and then went quiet.

Fox came along the bean row. Mouse came after, humming a small hum.

At the bend of the path, the tall flowers stood together, shoulder to shoulder, leaning their heads the other way. Behind them, in a bit of plain dirt, grew one little dandelion. Dandelion held her white fluff tight and stayed very still, the way small ones do when nobody makes room.

Fox stopped. Mouse stopped.

"There is room here," said Fox, gentle as a blanket.

"There is room here," said Mouse.

And they sat down in the warm dirt, one on each side of her, and they said the thing they always say.

"Right beside you, soft and slow."

Dandelion opened her fluff a little. The evening leaned in, warm.

Then the snail came, slow as syrup, and settled by her stem.

"Right beside you, soft and slow," said Fox and Mouse and the snail.

Then a beetle came, round and shiny, and tucked in under a leaf.

"Right beside you, soft and slow," said Fox and Mouse and the snail and the beetle.

Then two crickets came and folded their song down to a hum. Then a sparrow came down from the fence and made a feather pile of herself. Then the tall flowers turned their heads back around, all together, and bowed low over Dandelion like a soft green roof.

So the whole garden was one warm pile, with Dandelion in the middle of it, and nobody was alone. Her stem stood easy now, and her fluff went wide and white as a puff of breath.

The Moon came up over Hush Hollow, quiet and kind.

And the snail is asleep, and the beetle is asleep, and the crickets are asleep, all snug in the sleepy, sleepy garden.

And the sparrow is asleep, and the tall flowers are asleep, and Dandelion is asleep with her fluff gone soft, all snug in the sleepy, sleepy garden.

And Fox is asleep, and Mouse is asleep, one on each side, warm hand to warm hand, all snug in the sleepy, sleepy garden.

Right beside you, soft and slow.

Goodnight, Dandelion. Goodnight, tall flowers. Goodnight, snail and beetle and crickets and sparrow. Goodnight, Moon.

Goodnight, Fox. Goodnight, Mouse.

Goodnight, Hush Hollow.
