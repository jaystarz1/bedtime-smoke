Goodnight by the Stream

In Hush Hollow, at the end of a long soft day, Fox and Mouse went down to the stream.

The water went by slow and slow and slow. The reeds leaned over and hushed.

"I want to stay near you tonight," said Mouse.

"Then stay near," said Fox. "That is easy. That is the easiest thing there is."

Mr. Trout came up from under the smooth grey stones. His silver fins turned. His long tail curled a ring in the quiet water.

"Sit," said Mr. Trout, in his round bubbling voice. "The Hollow is going to sleep. I will tell you who."

So Fox and Mouse sat on the warm flat rock with a blanket over their knees.

"The reeds go first," said Mr. Trout.

And the reeds are asleep in the soft green stream. Loved and safe. Loved and safe.

"Then the ducks," said Mr. Trout. Three small brown ducks tucked their heads under their wings.

And the ducks are asleep, and the reeds are asleep in the soft green stream. Loved and safe. Loved and safe.

"Then the frogs," said Mr. Trout. The frogs stopped their little songs, one by one, and settled in the cool green mud.

And the frogs are asleep, and the ducks are asleep, and the reeds are asleep in the soft green stream. Loved and safe. Loved and safe.

"Then the stones," said Mr. Trout. "Stones sleep too. Stones are very good at it."

Mouse laughed, small and sleepy, and leaned on Fox. Fox held Mouse's hand and did not let go.

And the stones are asleep, and the frogs are asleep, and the ducks are asleep, and the reeds are asleep in the soft green stream. Loved and safe. Loved and safe.

"And then me," said Mr. Trout. He sank down slow, under the smooth grey stones, and his silver tail waved once, and was still.

Fox and Mouse went home along the path, slow and slow and slow, back through the warm grass of Hush Hollow, to the little bed with the quilt turned down.

"Say the whole thing," said Mouse.

So Fox said it soft:

And Mr. Trout is asleep, and the stones are asleep, and the frogs are asleep, and the ducks are asleep, and the reeds are asleep in the soft green stream.

And Mouse is asleep. And Fox is asleep.

Loved and safe. Loved and safe.

Goodnight, Mr. Trout. Goodnight, stream. Goodnight, Moon. Goodnight, Fox. Goodnight, Mouse.

Goodnight, Hush Hollow.
