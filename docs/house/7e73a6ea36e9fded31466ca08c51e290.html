The Lost Duckling by the Water

In Hush Hollow, down where the reeds go soft, the Pond lay still and dark. Bunny and Hedgehog sat close together by the water, warm and cozy.

"It's so dark tonight," said Hedgehog, in a small voice.

"I'm here," said Bunny. "Soft and safe. I'm right here."

Then a tiny sound came up from the reeds. A little duckling, all alone, far from home.

"I'm lost," peeped the duckling. "I can't find my way."

Hedgehog leaned close to Bunny. "Can we help?"

"We can," said Bunny. "Slowly. Together."

So they went along the edge of the Pond, side by side, with the little duckling between them.

Soft and slow, soft and slow.

The dark was quiet and kind. Small glows lit the reeds, tiny lights blinking gold. Bunny held Hedgehog's hand, warm and steady.

"I'm here," said Bunny. "Soft and safe."

Up ahead, the water rippled. Mr. Trout rose from the deep, his silver back agleam, his eyes calm and slow.

"A little duckling, lost?" rumbled Mr. Trout. "Home is just past the bending reeds, where the warm nest waits. Follow the still water. I'll light the way."

And Mr. Trout swam ahead, low and gleaming, a soft silver line through the dark.

Soft and slow, soft and slow.

Hedgehog was not so worried now. The Pond was gentle. The Moon rested above, quiet and pale, and the stars kept their watch.

Around the bending reeds they went, past the sleepy lily pads, past the hush of the water.

And there, a warm nest, glowing soft. A mother duck, waiting.

"Home," peeped the little duckling, and settled in close, warm and safe at last.

"Goodnight, little duckling," said Bunny.

Soft and slow, soft and slow.

Bunny and Hedgehog walked back through the Hollow, hand in hand.

"You helped," said Bunny. "You brought her home."

Hedgehog gave a slow, sleepy smile.

Back by the reeds, they curled up cozy, warm and still. The dark was soft now. The dark was kind.

"I'm here," said Bunny. "Soft and safe. Sleep now."

Now it is time for the goodnights. Goodnight, Hedgehog. Goodnight, Bunny. Goodnight, Mr. Trout, down in the deep. Goodnight, little duckling. Goodnight, Moon. Goodnight, Hush Hollow.
