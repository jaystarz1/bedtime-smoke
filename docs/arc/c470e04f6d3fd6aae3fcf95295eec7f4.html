Goodnight, Hush Hollow

Read in a Hush Hollow house voice · ages 2–5

Part of your first week at Hush Hollow.

In Hush Hollow, where the grass grew soft and the Wood grew quiet, Fox and Mouse sat close together at the edge of the meadow. The evening was warm. The evening was slow. Far above the Wood, the Moon was rising.

Near a leaf, a little snail named Dew had lost her way. Her small shell trembled. "I cannot find my home," she said, soft and worried. "It is by the round white stone, and I am so far away."

"We will help you," said Fox. "We will take you there," said Mouse. So Dew climbed onto a wide green leaf, and Fox held one side, and Mouse held the other, and together they began to go.

Soft and slow, soft and slow, they carried little Dew across the meadow.

They went past the sleepy clover. They went past the nodding daisies. They went past the quiet puddle where the frogs had hushed. Robin hopped down beside them, fluffed her feathers, and sang a small good-evening song. "I will show you the way," she chirped, and she fluttered just ahead, low to the ground, her wings a quiet flutter in the dusk.

Soft and slow, soft and slow, they followed Robin through the grass.

The meadow grew dim and gentle. The grass smelled sweet and cool. Fireflies blinked here and there. And there, at last, by the round white stone, was Dew's small cozy home. "We are here," said Mouse. "You are home," said Fox.

Dew slid down from the leaf and curled into her shell, warm and safe and glad. Her little shell rested warm in the dark. "Thank you," she whispered. "Goodnight."

Then Fox and Mouse and Robin turned back toward the Hollow.

Soft and slow, soft and slow, they walked home under the wide quiet sky.

Back in Hush Hollow, the grass was soft. Their eyes grew heavy. Robin tucked her head beneath her wing.

"Goodnight, Fox," said Mouse, with a yawn. "Goodnight, Mouse," said Fox, soft and low. "Goodnight, Robin," they whispered, and Robin gave one small sleepy chirp.

Goodnight, little Dew, safe by your stone. Goodnight, Moon. Goodnight, stars. Goodnight, Hush Hollow.

This is the house voice. On a voice tier, the very same story is read in your voice, with your child's name woven in.
