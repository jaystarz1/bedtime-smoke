The Flower That Waits for Night

Read in a Hush Hollow house voice · ages 2–5

Part of your first week at Hush Hollow.

In the Garden, the day goes soft and dim. Here is the warm stone wall. Here is the watering can, tipped on its side, asleep. Here is the little green bud that only opens at night.

Hedgehog and Bunny sat down close to wait for it. "When will it open?" asked Bunny, very small. "Slowly," said Dandelion, snug in her shell. "The best things come slow."

So they waited. They did not hurry. A moth came by, soft as dust. A cricket tuned one quiet string. Here is the snail-trail, silver in the dusk. Here is the last little bee, gone home to bed. The first star opened in the dark, the way the flower would.

"Still slow," said Dandelion. "Slow and soon. Slow and soon."

They waited while the sky went from gold to grey to deep soft blue. They waited while the whole Garden breathed out, and went still. Bunny leaned on Hedgehog. Hedgehog leaned on Bunny. Neither one hurried the night.

Here is the cool that comes when the light goes. Here is the hush the Garden makes, settling leaf by leaf. A folded poppy. A drowsy worm gone down to bed. A last small petal closing for the night. Nothing in the Garden hurried — and so neither did they.

Slow and soon. Slow and soon.

Then, little by little, the green bud loosened. One soft petal. Then another. Then another. And there it was — the night flower, open and pale and sweet, glowing like a small white moon down in the grass.

"Oh," breathed Bunny. "It was worth the wait." "It always is," said Dandelion, slow and kind.

The Garden was quiet. The night flower glowed. Hedgehog gave a slow yawn. Bunny gave a slower one. Slow and soon. Slow and soon.

Now it is time for the goodnights. Goodnight, night flower. Goodnight, Dandelion, snug in her shell. Goodnight, warm wall. Goodnight, sleepy watering can. Goodnight, Hedgehog. Goodnight, Bunny. Goodnight, Hush Hollow.

This is the house voice. On a voice tier, the very same story is read in your voice, with your child's name woven in.
