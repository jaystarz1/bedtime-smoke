When the Dark Came Soft

Read in a Hush Hollow house voice · ages 2–5

Part of your first week at Hush Hollow.

The day folded up its light, and the Wood went soft and dim. Fox and Mouse sat close together by the great old tree, where the dark was growing.

The dark was big tonight. It filled the spaces between the trees. It sat deep in the hollows. "I don't think I like the dark," said Fox, very small. "Me neither," said Mouse, smaller still.

Then out from the den came Bear — big, and slow, and warm, with kind eyes. "Hello, little ones," Bear rumbled, low and gentle. "Are you afraid of the dark?" "A little," said Fox and Mouse together. "Come and see," said Bear. "The dark is soft and safe."

So the three went into the Wood, Bear ambling slow between them. And up close, the dark was not empty at all. There were fireflies, little and gold, blinking on and off. There was the Moon, round and full, and the whole Wood lay silver and soft and safe. There was the warm glow of Bear's den, deep and golden and snug.

"Oh," said Mouse. "It's full of soft, small lights." "It isn't scary at all," said Fox. "It is soft and safe," said Bear. "Soft and safe."

Fox and Mouse took one small step into the dark, and then another. The dark did not bite. The dark did not roar. It was cool and quiet and kind, like a soft blanket laid over the whole wide Wood.

"We're not afraid anymore," said Fox and Mouse. "I knew you wouldn't be," said Bear. "Brave little ones. Soft and safe."

Then Bear gathered them in, close and warm, into the den where there is always room for one more. Fox settled down on one side. Mouse settled down on the other. The den was warm. Outside, the dark was kind now, full of soft and quiet light.

Fox gave a big, slow yawn. Mouse gave a smaller one. The fireflies blinked low. The dark lay soft and safe over the whole Wood.

Now it is time for the goodnights. Goodnight, Fox. Goodnight, Mouse. Goodnight, Bear, and the warm, deep den. Goodnight, little fireflies. Goodnight, moon. Soft and safe. Soft and safe. Goodnight, Hush Hollow.

This is the house voice. On a voice tier, the very same story is read in your voice, with your child's name woven in.
